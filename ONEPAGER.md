## The problem

Brands will spend ~£21B / $26B globally on influencer marketing this year, and the market is growing ~15% a year. Yet:

- **80% of brands decide their influencer spend on gut feel**, not data.
- **1 in 3 creators** has a fake-follower rate above 25% — invisible without the right tools.
- Existing tools dump 30+ vanity metrics on the buyer and leave the hard part — *"is this creator actually right for us?"* — unanswered.

The result: wasted budgets, mismatched campaigns, and no confident way to choose.

## The solution

Vettd turns scattered creator data into a decision:

- **Vettd Score (0–100)** — one transparent, weighted score per creator (engagement, audience authenticity, brand-fit, audience quality, consistency, growth).
- **Brand–Product Market Fit** — scores *your specific product* against a creator's audience, and recommends better-matched creators when the fit is weak.
- **Audience Overlap detector** — warns when two creators share the same followers, so brands don't pay twice for the same reach.
- **Side-by-side comparison**, demographics, ROI prediction, and an auto-generated campaign brief.

**Positioning:** not another database — a *decision engine*. "A decision, not a spreadsheet."
